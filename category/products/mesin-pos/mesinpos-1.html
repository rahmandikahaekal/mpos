FANLESS FULL FLAT TOUCH SCREEN POS SYSTEM

TP-2515
POS System TP-2515

The TP-2515 is a Fanless POS System with Modular Mechanism, Easy-Maintenance and Fashion Style. By introducing the low power consumption J1900 Quad-Core processor, TP-2515 can perform full-time tasks with the merits of less power consumption and noise-free in most of the applications.

The Modular Mechanical Design provides TP-2515 with easy maintenance especially on-site service. The hard disk drive, MSR modular, Customer Display and 2nd LCD Display can swiftly install or replaced with little efforts.

Easy-Maintenance, Noise-Free, and Reliability, TP-2515 is your cost-and-performance POS Hardware solution.
